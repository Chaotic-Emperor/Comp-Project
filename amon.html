Background

Amon is a natural born Mythical Creature with the Uniqueness of the Error Pathway. He is the Son of the Ancient True god and has lived for more than 2 milleniums. He is a true god of mischief and has made name for himself. During the ancient period, he even went as far as establishing a family with all the people being himself. With his natural born ability that he has full control of, he is also able to steal concepts such as day and night or distance as he wills without major backlashes.

Appearance

Amon stands at 1.8 meters with black eyes and curly black hair. He usually sports a monocle over His right eye with a mage robe and a black pointed hat.

Personality

Amon is a pleasure seeker in life who chooses to go out of his way to create chaos just so that revel in it later. He once went as far as waiting for a whole millenium just so that he come prank whoever came to open the Tomb. Being a born Mythical Creature, for him, humans are an inferior creature. With the help of his ability, being able to parasitise even the microbes in the year or completely steal the identity of a person, he is more than capable of pulling his pranks and guaranteeing success.
