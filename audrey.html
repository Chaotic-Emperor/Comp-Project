Background

Audrey is a noble and comes from an influential family. With her father as an earl and with say over the governmental decisions, she grew up not having to worry about money whatsoever leading to ignorance in the difficult lives of the people living below the poverty line. Being the youngest child in her family, she was sheltered by everyone else and didn't have to worry about taking over the family either leading to a carefree and blissful life.

Appearance

Audrey is a royal standing at 1.68 meters with blonde hair and emerald green eyes. She is noted to be an extremely beautiful person capable of attracting the attention of all the people that she comes across and being the daughter of a banker, she dresses rich. She is also known as the most Dazzling Gem of Backlund

Personality

Audrey is a young and inexperienced lady but someone fairly intelligent and capable of deducing a lot of details. She exudes a lot of joy and always makes sure to express her positivity when interacting with others going as far as taking the initiative to greet everyone she meets with a smile, including a supposed God. She always displays loyalty to the Tarot Club and loves her life. She took the step to recommend her friends in the tarot club so that she wouldn't have to maintain secrets with other people.
After requesting Mr. World to give her information on the suffering of the people, she is shown to be quite compassionate to those that are in suffering. Being a psychiatrist, she is also capable of helping others move on with their lives and solve any mental issues. She has also shown that she is capable of seeing through any disguises that people put forward making her capable of understanding the inner core of any person that she meets.
