Background

Alger Wilson is a member of Church of Lord of the Storms and also the Hanged Man of the Tarot Card Gathering. He is a sailor who commands the Blue Avenger, a Ghost Ship lost in the ancient times, which is won over by secretly killing the commander. It was at the same time that he came to be dragged into the Tarot Club.
Later on, Alger Wilson prayed to the The Fool in order to subdue the wild artifacts which caused him to lose his position as the Priest of Church of Lord of the Storms. He was then granted a position in the newly establed Church of the Fool by the Fool Himself

Appearance

Alger is a mixed blood with the characteristic trait of blue hair from the seas. He is in his 30s and usually hiding his identity while carrying out his tasks. It has also been noted in the novel that alger has bronze skin tone and appears to have been involved in a lot of battles

Personality

Alger has absolute loyalty to Mr. Fool. He unconditionally chooses to follow His commands and does not desire to test him, as show from his inner monologues of not regretting choosing to test him

Thou shan't test God

Alger is an experienced sailor with quite a good understanding of how the minds of different people work. He is even capable of giving other people advice while still being able to convince others that his conclusions are not wrong. Being very shrewd, he is also able to get positions of authority and maintain it.
