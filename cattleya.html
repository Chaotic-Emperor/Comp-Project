Background

Cattleya was an unknown child who was adopted by Queen Mystic, Bernadette Gustav, when she was still a child. Bernadette, being the daughter of Roselle Gustav, a transmigrator and a widely renowned emperor hailed for bringing about revolution, taught Cattleya the ins and outs of life with a proper knowledge of mysticism. Cattleya considers Bernadette to be her mother figure, teacher, and captain. She was later expelled from the fleet after which she created her own fleet and became renowned as a general of the seas.
Cattleya joined the Tarot Card gathering after being pulled above the grey fog by Mr Fool after she almost lost control from the murmurings of Hidden Sage. She then tried to test Mr Fool and glean into his identity but was largely reprimanded by him.

Appearance

Cattleya has black hair and purple eyes standing at a height of 1.66 meters. She wears a classical robe with a multitude of symbols on it and also has a lot of artifacts supplementing her strength. One of the major artifact that she carries with herself are the "Eyes of Mystery Prying" which allow her to supress her power and her all-seeing eyes

Personality

Cattleya appears to be a keen, observant, and cautious lady. She is a capable captain leading a massive fleet with a lot of subordinates at an equivalent strength. She is a curious enough beyonder that she chooses to try and pry into the secrets of many people and have enough weapons to use against other people which led her to pry into the secrets of Mr Fool as well.
It is later revealed that she is not as mature as she appears to be and still longs for a place in Bernadette's fleet. She wishes for he to come back and take her back to the ship waiting for her mother-figure. She has the mindset of a small child which she is able to supress most of the time.
